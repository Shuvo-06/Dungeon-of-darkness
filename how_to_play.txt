How to Play:

Movement:
  - Use the W key to move up.
  - Use the A key to move left.
  - Use the S key to move down.
  - Use the D key to move right.
  
Engaging in Combat:
  - When you're close to an Goblin (G), a duel will automatically begin. Keep an eye on health of both the player (P) and enemy to win the battle.
  - Multiple enemies can be in the dungeon at the same time, so be ready for multiple encounters!

Objective:
  - Your goal is to defeat a certain number of enemies before facing the final boss NEMESIS (N).
  - Once you defeat the final boss, you level up and earn special rewards.

Escaping the Game:
  - Press ESC at any time to terminate the game.
